US Road Accident Analytics: Unveiling Trends and Insights from US Road Data

Project Overview

This Business Intelligence and Data Analytics project analyzes over 3 million US road accident records to identify accident trends, risk factors, and opportunities to improve road safety.

The project combines data analysis, predictive analytics, machine learning, and Power BI visualization to support data-driven decision-making for policymakers, emergency services, and road safety organizations.

Objectives

* Analyze accident trends across time and locations
* Identify factors contributing to accident severity
* Detect accident hotspots
* Evaluate weather and environmental impacts
* Develop predictive models for accident severity
* Create interactive Power BI dashboards

Results

* Random Forest achieved the highest classification accuracy (90.73%)
* Identified peak accident periods during morning and evening rush hours
* Weather conditions significantly impacted accident severity
* Generated actionable road safety recommendations
